Settings¶

shopkit.stock.simple.settings.STOCK_CHOICES¶

Available choices for the stock field of StockedItems.

shopkit.stock.simple.settings.STOCK_DEFAULT¶

Default choice for the stock field of StockedItems.

shopkit.stock.simple.settings.STOCK_ORDERABLE¶

An iterable with choices from STOCK_CHOICES which represent orderable states – states which will not raise an exception when saving the shopping cart.
